Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Simply Fueled ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Simply Fueled mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from those providers.

Profile data you provide: Age, gender, fitness goal, activity level, dietary restrictions, and meal preferences you enter during onboarding.

Usage data: Number of meal plans generated per month, app interaction data, and session information.

Device information: Device type, operating system version, and anonymous crash reports to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized AI-powered meal plans tailored to your profile
  • To manage your account and subscription status
  • To enforce monthly plan generation limits
  • To prevent account sharing and abuse
  • To improve the app and fix bugs
  • To send important service notifications (not marketing without consent)

3. Artificial Intelligence

Simply Fueled uses OpenAI's API to generate personalized meal plans. Your profile data (age, goal, dietary restrictions, etc.) is sent to OpenAI's servers to generate your plan. OpenAI processes this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

We do not share personally identifiable information such as your name or email with OpenAI. Only the nutritional profile data necessary to generate your plan is transmitted.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), hosted in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication tokens.

Session tokens are used to enforce a single active session per account. Signing in on a new device automatically invalidates previous sessions.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database — Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: AI meal plan generation — Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management — Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In: Authentication — Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. Monthly usage counters are automatically reset on the 1st of each month. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Simply Fueled is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Withdraw consent for optional data processing

9. Not Medical Advice

⚠️ Important disclaimer: The meal plans, nutritional information, and content provided by Simply Fueled are generated by artificial intelligence and are intended for informational and educational purposes only.

This app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional, registered dietitian, or nutritionist before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you have any medical conditions, allergies, or are pregnant or nursing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
